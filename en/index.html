About

Illustration from Victor Hugo et son temps (1881) My research is in data-driven astrophysics and cosmology. This means I’m somewhere between being an astronomer and a physicist, somewhere between a theorist, observer and statistician. I also teach data science to astronomy PhD students as part of the Rubin Observatory Data Science Fellowship Program. Over the last century, we have discovered that the universe consists primarily of baryons (or “normal” matter), dark matter, and dark energy.
